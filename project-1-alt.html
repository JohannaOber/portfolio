EdTech Mobile

BIRD Education Platform

Mobile companion app for Germany's national education platform, helping students discover and navigate their options.

Role

UX & UI Designer

Client

Hochschule der Medien

Academic Project · Team of 2

2024

Challenge

Navigating Germany's educational landscape

BIRD (Bildungsraum Digital) is Germany's federally funded platform for discovering educational programs and institutions. Its web prototype needed a mobile companion to help students not just find offerings, but navigate them with confidence.

01 Discovery

User Research and Journey Mapping

We mapped the journey of education seekers and identified key moments of uncertainty and overwhelm. Interviews with high school students revealed a need for personalisation and guidance: not just a search engine, but a companion that understands where you are in life.

02 Define

Defining Features and Priorities

Based on our findings, we defined the core feature set: personalised content via the BIRD Wallet, intelligent search across all programs and institutions, a personal notebook for collecting favourites, and a chatbot offering real-time support.

03 Design

Wireframes to High-Fidelity

We developed wireframes for all core flows and iterated toward high-fidelity screens. The visual language, inspired by paper and handwriting, was chosen to feel familiar and approachable for younger users while communicating quality through texture and tactility.

04 Test

Usability Testing and Iteration

The interactive prototype was tested with students and educators. Feedback confirmed the value of the personalisation layer and the chatbot, and led to refinements in the notebook and search interactions. The paper-inspired visual style was consistently well-received.

Solution

One app, every step of the way

The app brings every program and institution in Germany into one place. The BIRD Wallet surfaces personalised content based on each user's interests. A notebook saves favourites; a chatbot answers questions on the go. The paper-inspired design adds warmth and familiarity, especially for first-timers navigating an unfamiliar system.

Impact

Clarity confirmed in testing

Usability tests with students and teachers praised the prototype for clarity and ease of navigation. The concept is now under consideration as part of the national BIRD platform strategy.
